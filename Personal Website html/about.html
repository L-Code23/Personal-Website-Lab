But Ramsey's story extends far beyond the brewery walls. A former college basketball player at the University of Massachusetts Lowell, Ramsey knows what it means to work hard, persevere, and strive for excellence both on and off the court. His time on the basketball court instilled in him a sense of discipline, teamwork, and resilience that continues to shape his approach to life's challenges.

In a testament to his adventurous spirit, Ramsey embarked on a remarkable journey, pedaling his way from the lush forests of Seattle to the sun-drenched shores of San Francisco. Through rain and shine, ups and downs, Ramsey's epic bicycle journey was a testament to his determination, endurance, and love for adventure. But Ramsey's thirst for knowledge and new experiences knows no bounds. Currently immersed in the world of C#, Ramsey is embracing the challenge with characteristic gusto, eager to expand his skill set and explore new horizons in the ever-evolving realm of technology.
